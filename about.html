About Us

Founded to bridge business between Qatar and Africa, GHB Consulting is a professional advisory platform led by a network of executives and entrepreneurs from the Greater Horn of Africa and beyond. Our members and partners share one goal: helping each other build sustainable, high-impact ventures across borders.

At GHB, we believe business success is built on trust, collaboration, and community. Whether you're entering new markets, refining your strategy, seeking strategic financial insights, or exploring investment opportunities, we offer the knowledge, relationships, and support to grow and scale.

From the Gulf to the Horn of Africa and across East Africa’s vibrant economies, we connect vision with execution, and ideas with measurable outcomes.
